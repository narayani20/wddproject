About us

As a worldwide organization, our main concern is to deliver healthcare on a global scale, particularly to those in greatest need.
We help them not only for their physical but also their mental, emotional and everyday requirements.
We give modern medicine for people who have endured both obvious and subtle injustices throughout history and in the current context.
We strictly object that any life is of lesser value than another.
